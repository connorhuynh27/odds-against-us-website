About the Show

The Odds Against Us is a podcast examining how modern gambling environments are shaping youth behavior. Each episode features conversations with experts, advocates, and people with personal experience in gambling and gambling prevention.

Topics covered include the rise of mobile and sports betting, the design mechanics platforms use to drive engagement, youth vulnerability in digital spaces, and the public health and policy questions raised by predatory gambling practices.

Hosted by Connor Huynh.
